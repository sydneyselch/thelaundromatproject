Sophie Henderson

Independent Arts Consultant

For more than 20 years, Sophie has worked with non-profit arts organizations on strategic planning, fundraising, board relations and donor cultivation, communications and audience engagement, and organizational management. She worked with Resnicow + Associates (1993-2008), an agency providing integrated communications, marketing, strategic planning, and management consulting services to cultural institutions, corporate sponsors of the arts, and government agencies. Institutional positions include the Museum of Arts and Design (2012-2014) and Creative Capital Foundation (2009-2012) where she led the External Affairs teams and served as part of the senior management teams. Now an independent consultant focusing on support for organizational outreach and operations, she is working on a long-term project with Basilica Hudson; other recent clients include The Aldrich Contemporary Art Museum, Creative Capital Foundation and the Corning Museum of Glass.
